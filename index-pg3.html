Human Effect on Species and Habitat Conservation

by Cameron Santiago for MMP 100-0901.


The Vaquita is the world's rarest marine mammal. Being on the edge of extinction, the porpoise wasn't discovered until the late twentieth century. Initially caught and drowned by those who fished them illegally, they are now under protected areas within Mexico. Despite protection, their population continues to fall.
