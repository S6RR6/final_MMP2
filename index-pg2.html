Human Effect on Species and Habitat Conservation

by Cameron Santiago for MMP 100-0901.


The Javan Rhinocerous is the most threated out of all rhincerous species. As of 2017, less than 100 remain, all of which live in captivity within a national park in Indonesia. With ongoing poaching, this rhinocerous species requires constant guards that have been in effect since 2010.
