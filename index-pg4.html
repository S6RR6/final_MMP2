Human Effect on Species and Habitat Conservation

by Cameron Santiago for MMP 100-0901.


The Amur Leopard is a subspecies that has since adapted to life in forests within the northern hemisphere. Oftentimes, they are poached for their fur and threatened by deforestation. Because of their inclusive nature, protection for the species is inconclusive.
